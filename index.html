About Me

I work across both research and industry, combining academic depth with hands-on engineering. Over the years, I've contributed to published research in trustworthy healthcare AI and built production systems used by thousands of people. Some of my work includes developing the Zenyora AI wellness platform, creating a real-time RAG-based sales agent, and building M-TRUST, an open-source toolkit for fairness and explainability in medical AI.

I enjoy working on problems where accuracy alone is not enough—systems that need transparency, fairness, and real-world reliability. My current focus spans multimodal medical AI, bias mitigation, LLM-based decision support, and scalable ML systems for everyday use.

Growing up in rural Bangladesh where healthcare access was limited, I saw firsthand the need for democratizing medical care and education. This experience led me to found OgroPath, an AI-powered platform that makes medical entrance exam preparation accessible to students across Bangladesh, especially in underserved communities.

Whether in research labs or production environments, I aim to build AI that people can trust—and that genuinely improves lives.
